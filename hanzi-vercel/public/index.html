Help & FAQ
How much does Melozi cost? ▾
Melozi Pro is $5.99/month or $49.99/year (save ~30%). Both plans include unlimited songs, all three languages, real-time AI translations, and your full vocabulary deck. You get a 7-day free trial to start — no credit card required during beta.
How do I cancel? ▾
Cancel anytime from Profile → Manage Subscription. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period. No questions asked, no cancellation fees.
Why do I need Spotify? ▾
Spotify tells Melozi which song you're currently playing so lyrics sync automatically — no searching needed. You can also tap any song in the Popular list and Melozi starts it on Spotify for you. Spotify Premium is required for remote playback control.
Why isn't my song loading? ▾
Melozi fetches synced lyrics from LRCLIB, a community lyrics database. Not every song has synced lyrics yet — major artists in Mandarin, Korean, and Japanese have the best coverage. Try the exact song title if a search fails.
The pinyin or romanization looks off ▾
AI translation is very good but not perfect — tones on multi-character words can occasionally be combined incorrectly. Tap ⋯ in the player and use "Re-translate this song" to try again. We're continuously improving accuracy.
Will my saved words sync across devices? ▾
Cross-device sync is coming soon with Melozi accounts. For now your deck is saved locally — install Melozi to your home screen to keep it safe and avoid losing your words if you clear your browser.
How do I install Melozi on my phone? ▾
iPhone: tap Share in Safari → "Add to Home Screen." Android: tap the menu (⋮) → "Add to Home Screen." This gives you a full-screen app experience with no browser bar, and keeps your data safe between sessions.
